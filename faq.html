FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

If the answers to your questions can not be found here, please contact us directly by clicking here.

How many locations does CakeTown currently have?

CakeTown currently has two locations in Vancouver. Directions and contact information for our Knight Street Location can be found here, and our Oak Street Location, here. We plan to expand our company in the near future by opening more locations in the Lower Mainland.

What kind of products does CakeTown offer?

CakeTown is proud to offer a wide variety of products! We make everything from cakes and cupcakes, to even pastries. Our full list of products can be found here.

How long does delivery usually take?

CakeTown believes that fast delivery is essential for providing excellent customer service. We deliver all of our products within the same day across Metro Vancouver, and offer varying speeds of delivery for locations outside of Metro Vancouver. Our full list of delivery fees can be found during the checkout process.
